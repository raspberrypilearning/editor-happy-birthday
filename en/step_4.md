<h2 class="c-project-heading--task">Resize images</h2>

--- task ---

Change the size of the image with CSS code.

--- /task ---

--- task ---

Click on the file icon and go to the `style.css` file. 

![screenshot](images/step4.png)

--- /task ---

--- task ---

Go to the `#pic` part of the CSS code. Change the `width` and `height` of the outside image to `200px` 

--- /task ---
<div class="c-project-code">
--- code ---
---
language: css
filename: style.css
line_numbers: true
line_number_start: 15
line_highlights: 16-17
---
#pic {
  width: 200px;
  height: 200px;
}
--- /code ---

--- task ---

Click **Run** to see the new size. Keep playing with the size until you are happy with how it looks.

--- /task ---
</div>
